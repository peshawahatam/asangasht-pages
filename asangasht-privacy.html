Gashty Asan Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AsanGasht may collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

Information We May Collect

  • Location information when you choose to use location-based features
  • Photos you select when uploading images in the app
  • Basic account or booking-related information you provide while using the service

How We Use Information

  • To provide app features and improve the user experience
  • To help users discover places, listings, and related content
  • To support booking, listing, and communication features
  • To respond to support requests and improve app reliability

Location and Photos

Gashty Asan requests access to your location to support place-related features and requests access to your photo library so you can upload images when using the app. These permissions are used only for relevant app functionality.

Data Sharing

Gashty Asan does not sell your personal information. Information may be used only as needed to operate and improve the app.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Account information and booking data are kept while your account is active. If you stop using the app, we may retain minimal data as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. To submit a deletion request:

  • Email us at: support@gashtyasan.com
  • Include your account email and the request to delete your data
  • We will process your request within 30 days

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at privacy@asangasht.app.
